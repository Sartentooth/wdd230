Rental Facilities

Playa del Carmen Branch

Discover the thrill of exploring Cozumel from our Playa del Carmen branch, strategically located near the ferry. Here, we offer a wide range of vehicles to make your adventure unforgettable. From Honda scooters ideal for 1 or 2 persons to Honda Side-by-Side ATVs accommodating 4 persons. Prefer the versatility of a Jeep Wrangler? We have models with 2 and 4 doors, perfect for 4 and 5 occupants respectively. Choose from our flexible rental options: half-day (3 hours) or full day. Additionally, we streamline your experience by providing both advance reservations and walk-in rentals directly at our branch.

Near Puerta Maya Terminal Branch

If your point of entry is the Puerta Maya Terminal, our second branch awaits you with the same quality of service and a variety of vehicles. Whether you want to explore as a couple or in a group, we have Honda scooters, Side-by-Side ATVs, and Jeep Wranglers to meet all your needs. Just like in Playa del Carmen, at this location, we offer tailored rental options: half-day or full day. Whether you prefer to plan ahead with an advance reservation or decide on the spot with a walk-in rental, we are here to make your experience in Cozumel unforgettable.
