About Us

Welcome to the island paradise of Cozumel, where the sea breeze blends with the excitement of exploring the island on our Honda scooters and Jeep Wranglers. Ready to discover every corner of the island in style? You’re in the right place! With two strategically located rental facilities in El Centro and at the Puerta Maya terminal, we make access to fun easy for you. Whether you choose the agility of a scooter or the sturdiness of a Jeep, your journey begins here. Unwind and immerse yourself in the beauty of Cozumel. Rent with us and let the adventure begin!

Cozumel is known for its stunning coral reefs, which are home to an unparalleled diversity of marine life. Our vehicles will allow you to reach the island’s best diving and snorkeling spots with ease. Imagine the thrill of swimming alongside sea turtles, rays, and a burst of colorful tropical fish.

In addition, Cozumel has a rich Mayan history and culture that is worth exploring. With our Jeeps, you can venture into the ancient Mayan ruins, such as San Gervasio, and learn about the fascinating history of this ancient civilization. Or perhaps you prefer to stroll through the vibrant market of El Centro, where you can shop for local crafts and sample delicious Mexican cuisine.

But that’s not all. Cozumel also offers a variety of outdoor activities, from hiking and cycling to bird watching and fishing. Our vehicles are perfect for taking you on these adventures, offering you the freedom to explore at your own pace. At the end of the day, nothing compares to watching the sunset over the Caribbean Sea from the comfort of your Jeep. It’s the perfect end to a day full of adventures.

So, what are you waiting for? Rent with us and discover all that Cozumel has to offer. We look forward to welcoming you and helping you start your island adventure!

Rental Facilities

El Centro Cozumel Branch

Discover the thrill of exploring Cozumel from our El Centro Cozumel Branch near to Playa del Carmen, strategically located near the ferry. Here, we offer a wide range of vehicles to make your adventure unforgettable.

From Honda scooters ideal for 1 or 2 persons to Honda Side-by-Side ATVs accommodating 4 persons. Prefer the versatility of a Jeep Wrangler? We have models with 2 and 4 doors, perfect for 4 and 5 occupants respectively.

Choose from our flexible rental options: half-day (3 hours) or full day. Additionally, we streamline your experience by providing both advance reservations and walk-in rentals directly at our branch.

Puerta Maya Terminal Branch

If your point of entry is the Puerta Maya Terminal, our second branch awaits you with the same quality of service and a variety of vehicles. Whether you want to explore as a couple or in a group, we have Honda scooters, Side-by-Side ATVs, and Jeep Wranglers to meet all your needs.

Just like in El Centro, at this location, we offer tailored rental options: half-day or full day. Whether you prefer to plan ahead with an advance reservation or decide on the spot with a walk-in rental, we are here to make your experience in Cozumel unforgettable.
